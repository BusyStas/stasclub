Fidelity Crypto

Here is how you can invest in Bitcoin and Ethereum through Fidelity:

  • You need to have a reguylar brokerage account first. It will be used as a parent for the crypto account
  • Then you need to open a new account and choose "Fidelity Crypto" as the type of account
  • You will not get any private keys, wallets or seed phrases. They will custody all your assets by themselves.
  • Currently, you can only trade BTC, ETH and LTC.
  • You cannot deposit or withdraw crypto, but you can sell it to cash and withdraw cash
