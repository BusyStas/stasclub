Crypto Exchanges

Everyone starts here, because only an exchange allows you take money from a bank account and buy crypto. When you use a crypto exchange (unlike a wallet), you don't need to worry about loosing a seed phrase or forgetting a password. All crypto exchanges require you to go through KYC (Know Your Customer) process, which means you need to provide your ID and SSN, like any other bank. Exchanges all offer a good APR% and you can earn income for holding your crypto like in a savings account. Exchanges will likely send you a 1099 tax form for your tax return (IRS).

Crypto Wallets

Wallets are for advanced users. You cannot participate in Token Pre-Sale if you don't have a wallet, because they all use "Connect Wallet" and you cannot use an exchange for this. You need to use a wallet if you want to earn money in crypto projects. You are responsible for retaining your seed phrase, because it cannot be changed likt a password. It is very similar to an encrypted disk drive, if you lose the key, you cannot recover what is in it. Unlike exchanges, wallets do not give you APR% for holding tokens.

Earn Money

DePIN projects give you passive income. You can earn $1000 per year by driving your car. You can earn $50 for sharing your GPU power, and so on. Most DePIN projects require you having a wallet before you begin, while some will create a wallet for you.
