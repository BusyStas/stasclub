Crypto Exchanges

Everyone starts here, because you need to use one of exchanges to bring your money in from a bank account. When you use a crypto exchange (unlike a wallet), you don't need to worry about loosing a seed phrase or forgetting a password. All crypto exchanges require you to go through KYC (Know Your Customer) process, which means you need to provide your ID and SSN, like any other bank.

Crypto Wallets

Wallets are for advanced users. You are responsible for retaining your seed phrase, because it cannot be changed likt a password. It is very similar to an encrypted disk drive, if you lose the key, you cannot recover what is in it.
