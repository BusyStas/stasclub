Helium Mobile

Helium Mobile is a new mobile network that leverages community wifi routers to offload mobile networks. You can earn $HNT by sharing your wifi router with the Helium network, and they sell bandwith to mobile operators like AT&T and Verizon. Don't be confused - there is Helium Mobile (a mobile network) and Helium Deploy (a decentralized network for IoT devices). You can start with getting a free SIM card and free phone plan.

DIMO

I have a DIMO device and I earn $DIMO tokens for sharing my car data. This device is connected to the OBD2 port of my car and collects information about my car’s performance. You might earn $500 to $1,000 per year, depending on the token price—and if you have a Tesla, you may earn even more. It’s not cash that you receive; you earn tokens, and their value can grow over time. I was amazed to see customer services actually replying and helping.

MapMetrics

MapMetrics is a decentralized navigation app that rewards users for sharing their location data to help build a precise and community-driven map. You can earn $MMT tokens by using the app while driving or walking. You don’t need to keep the app open—just click “Connect” once, and it will run in the background. I can use this app at the same time as DIMO. Typically, you earn around 150 tokens per day, although the token price may vary.

XForge Phone

You can run a Xardian Node on your phone and earn $XFORGE tokens. The XForge and Xardian ecosystem uses a native utility token—typically referred to as $XARD (or a similarly branded token)—to power its decentralized infrastructure and reward users. You can earn tokens in several ways: by running a Xardian Node, which provides compute or network services; by actively using an XForge Phone, which contributes data, interacts with dApps, or serves as a control point for nodes; and by participating in staking, governance, or referrals. Users are rewarded based on the value and reliability of their contributions to the network, whether it's bandwidth, compute cycles, uptime, or trusted participation.
