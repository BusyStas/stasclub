What is a Pre-Sale (ICO)?

Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs) allow investors to participate in early-stage cryptocurrency projects. Learn how to identify promising ICOs and invest wisely. These are the most risky investments, because they may never reach the production ready condition, or when that business goes production, it may become ignored by the users, and eventually go bankruptcy. Always do your own research, and expect a good percentage of these project to fail.

What is guaranteed is that it will take years before you see any profit from it. Here is a typical timeline:

  • 1-2 years: Pre-sale during the development and testing of the project. Price changes with phases.
  • 3-4 years: Initial launch and limited adoption. Token may appear on DEX (traded over the counter).
  • 5+ years: Potential for significant growth and market acceptance. Token may appear on Exchanges.
